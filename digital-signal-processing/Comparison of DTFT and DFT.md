- ## DFT Is Sampled DTFT
	- DFT is periodic in $k$ with period N
	- DFT frequency variable is discrete, where
		- $\omega=\Omega T_{s}=\frac{2\pi k}{N}$
		- $k=N$ at $\omega=2\pi$ and at $f=f_{s}$
		- DFT is complex
