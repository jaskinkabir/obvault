- ## General Approach to Signal Manipulation
	- We have an original signal $f(t)$
	- We can create a new signal $g(t) = f(at+t_{0})$
		- The factor a causes a scaling (shrinking for a>0)
		- The factor $t_{0}$ causes a horizontal shift
