- ## Arithmetic Mean
	- The **Sample Arithmetic Mean:**
		- Computed using sample data
			- Symbol: $\overline{X}$
			- This is a statistic
			- For $n$ individuals:
				- $\overline{X} = \frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^nX_{i}$
	- The **Population Mean**
		- Symbol: $\mu$
