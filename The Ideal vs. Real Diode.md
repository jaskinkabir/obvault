- ## Ideal Diode
	- Short circuit for + voltage
	- Open circuit for - voltage
- ## Real Diode
	- Current follows an s curve
	- I-V curve consists of 3 characteristic regions
		- Forward bias $V>0$
		- Reverse bias $V<0$
		- Breakdown $V\ll0$
